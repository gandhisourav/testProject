							PROJECT INTRODUCTION
This project is going to unite all those players who love to play cricket and miss playing that as they are far away from their home countries.


							PROJECT FUNCTIONS
1.   	Takes availability of people to play cricket.
2.   	Combines the availabilities of people and group them.
3.   	Random selection of playground.
4.   	Collection of funds for the equipment. 
	(Totally upto players how much they want to contribute)
5.   	Inform players about the schedule.
6.   	Scoring.
7.   	Special appreciation to those who perform well.
